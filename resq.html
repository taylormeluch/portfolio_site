What We Know

Approximately 3.3 million dogs enter animal shelters nationwide every year.
-ASPCA


Over 2 million puppies are bred in puppy mills each year.
-The Puppy Mill Project

But... why?


If there is such a large number of dogs in shelters, why do people support puppy mills breeding more dogs, potentially adding to the problem?

- Kevin, Rescue Dog Owner

Problem Statement

The dog overpopulation problem stems from eager adopters being deterred by the lengthy process of adopting through rescue groups.

Affinity Mapping

Potential adopter needs and wants: 

Faster adoption application approval

Better communication and status updates during adoption process

User Flow Before Solution

The Solution

Easy, quick, organized way to apply to adopt rescue dogs with status updates, easy messaging with rescue group, and shorter approval wait times.
